Project Description:

Our weather forecast app provides users with up-to-date weather information and forecasts for the selected location. It aims to deliver accurate weather data through a user-friendly interface.

App Goals:

  • Accuracy: Ensure high accuracy in weather forecasts through reliable data sources and advanced algorithms.
  • User-friendly Interface: Design an intuitive and accessible interface for all users regardless of their technical backgrounds.
  • Responsive Design: The app includes dynamic background images and a consistent design, enhancing the overall look and user experience.
  • Real-time Updates: Provide timely updates and alerts based on changing weather conditions.
  • Localization: Offer localized weather forecasts tailored to the user's geographic location.
